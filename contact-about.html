About Artist

As a child I always found myself painting nature and animals. With the true passion to all the living things surrounding us, I returned back to painting water colors already as an adult. Learning world through the painting is truly amazing.
You look deeper into nature, how animals and birds behave, their joyful hopping and running, singing and flying, these all I am happily expressing through my paintings.
